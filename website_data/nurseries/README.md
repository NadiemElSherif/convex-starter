## Why Potty Train at Your Nursery?

### Build confidence and prevent health issues

Getting potty trained is a big step for children towards independence. They no longer rely on their caretakers to change them and wipe their bums. They can do it themselves! A big boost for their confidence and self esteem. And once children have tackled the potty training hurdle, they have more space to develop other cognitive and motoric skills.

Besides that, if children start potty training late (after 3 years) they have a higher chance at health issues such as constipation and bed wetting.

### Reduce workload and save on nappy costs

### Happy and healthy planet for our children

Stimulating parents to start potty training on time can lead to a 30% to 50% reduction in your nappy waste and related CO2 emissions.
